Making PBS Sandwich

  1. Get a plate.
  2. Get peanut butter knife.
  3. Get 2 slices of bread,peanut butter, and strawberry [jam].
  4. Use knife to spread strawberry on one slice of bread.
  5. Lick knife clean.
  6. Use same knife to spread peanut butter on 2nd slice of bread.
  7. You guessed it, lick the peanut butter off the knife, then put into sink to be cleaned.
  8. Combine the 2 slices together to complete your sandwich. Enjoy.
